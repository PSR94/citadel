# Platform On-Call Handover Guide

Use this guide during shift transition for SRE and platform engineering coverage.

## Handover content

- open incident summaries
- degraded service status and next checkpoints
- pending deployment freezes
- privileged access sessions still in progress

## Escalation reminder

If a Sev 1 incident remains open during handover, the incoming primary assumes incident commander responsibilities unless the duty manager explicitly appoints another lead.
