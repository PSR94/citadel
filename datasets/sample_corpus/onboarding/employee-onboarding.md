# Employee Onboarding Manual

Employees receive a managed workstation, corporate identity, payroll enrollment, and baseline access through role templates.

## Required steps

1. complete identity proofing with People Operations
2. accept security, privacy, and acceptable use policies
3. enroll a managed device in Device Trust
4. receive baseline role access through Access Broker
5. finish manager-approved team access within the first week

## Default entitlements

- payroll and HR system access
- collaboration suite access
- role-scoped engineering or business tooling access

## Differences from contractor onboarding

Employees are eligible for payroll, broader internal directory visibility, and long-lived managed device issuance. Contractors follow a narrower access profile and do not receive default production-adjacent engineering roles.
